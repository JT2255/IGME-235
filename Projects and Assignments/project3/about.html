Cool Guy Pixel Art

Cool Guy Pixel Art is a website where you can draw pixel art while also having the option to change the size of the grid. The user will also have the option to change colors and erase pixels as well.
Genre: Art
Platform: Web
Story: You want to be the coolest pixel artist so you use this website to practice
Aesthetics: Pixel Art Style
Gameplay: Clicking on pixels to draw and create images

Documentation

The process for this project was a long one as I could not figure out what I wanted to do for a while. After settling for a pixel art creator,
I got to work on thinking of what controls I could add for the website. Then it was about figuring out the layout and design of the buttons.
I settled on something simple that wouldn't be hard to understand while also being pleasing to the eye. For sound design, I went with a click
sound when selecting buttons that makes a users click feel impactful. I got the sound from here. I met the requirement of having a class by
using a class for the grid of pixels. It holds all of the information of the grid including the cells and their data. The website also looks good
on multiple ranges of displays because the controls use a flexbox layout that wraps when it hits the border of the page. Overall, I think the
project came out nice and I like how it turned out.
