Crucible

Crucible is the main player vs. player mode found in Destiny 2.
In crucible, there a multiple game modes to choose from which
will change the rules of the match. The available modes include
Control, Survival, and Clash. Control is a simple domination game
mode in which treams capture designated areas and earn points
by holding them. By reaching enough points the team will win.
Survival is a competitive game mode in Crucible where players fight
against each other with a limited amount of lives. When one team
runs out of live they lose the round. Clash is a simple team deathmatch
mode where player fight to reach a certain amount of kills for victory.
