Strikes

Strikes are a cooperative mode featured in Destiny 2.
Strikes represent a middle ground between story missions
and raids in terms of length and diffculty, and are optimized
for a fireteam of three players. Usually, Strikes are composed
for a linear series of enemy encounters which may or may
not include a mini-boss, culmination in a battle against one or
more bosses supported by additional enemies. Some strikes feature
simple puzzle mechanics that make boss fights more challenging.
