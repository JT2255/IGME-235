Gambit

Gambit is a player vs. player game mode in Destiny 2. Two teams
of four players are placed into two identical arenas. Players compete
to kill enemies and collect motes, which are deposited at a bank in the
center of the arena. Up to fifteen motes can be held at a time, and a
player will irretrievably lose all their motes if they die. Depositing at least
five, ten, or fifteen motes will spawn an enemy, dubbed a blocker, on the
other team's bank which they must kill to continue depositing motes.
On occasion, high-calue targets will appear, which are minibosses
differentiated by a white sparkling aura that drop a large amount of motes.
When a team fills up a bank with 75 motes, a boss will be summoned. By
killing this boss you will win the round. Each game of gambit is composed
of three rounds and the first to two rounds wins.
