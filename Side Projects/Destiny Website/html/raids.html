Raids

Raids are 6-player cooperative assassination missions that are
of high challenge, and require communication between players
to succceed. They involve specific quests for groups of fireteams.
No waypoints or explicit objectives are provided. Raids typically
involve climactic encounters agains various bosses, culmination
in intense final battles against legendary bosses. Raids also
compose of two difficulty levels, normal and prestige. A normal
mode raid must be completed before a prestige raid can be attempted.
Prestige mode provides tougher enemies, additional objectives, and a
harsher death penalty. There are a total of 6 raids in Destiny 2,
however only 3 are currently playable.
